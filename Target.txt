Trying to create the best todo app possible on free resources.

My goals:

1. Google Calendar synced in (both ways). Auto-Sync enabled by default(when access to internet)
2. Supported on web as well as on android app
3. Offline support in app
4. User can customize the Ui to its best and the way they want
5. Support on multiple devices with same id
6. Tracking down progress and helping building a habit
7. Tasks can be recurring, dependent, subtasks and even attachments can be added to them
8. Support for Pomodoro technique and time tracking
9. Notification on each task starting and way before the deadline to alert User
10. User can choose how early and how regularly he wants Notifications and also disable for tasks for which he does not need notifications
11. Establishing that TODOs/Tasks and Routine, both are different and hence handling both different ways
    -TODOs: Tasks with priority order and tags to identify at one glance which is urgent and important. Dependent on particular days and deadlines.
    -Routines: Tasks that are recurring and have no priority order. They are more like habits that need to be built and tracked down. Differ based on working and non-working days. Sunday is non-working by default. User can also make Saturday non-working by default. And if there is an unexpected holiday he can toggle it to a non-working day or change a working day to non-working one. The routine suggested and followed by the app for that day is hence different based on the type of day.
